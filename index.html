H aving always dreamed of working in an environment that represented her love for art and design as well as hair, Kari Lisak created Moxie. It started out as a boutique salon with four stations. Word spread quickly of the unique salon and within a few months grew from a staff of four stylists to eight. With the blessing of having more stylists and business than our space could handle, Kari made the choice to expand to the building next door and more than double the space Moxie now has 10 stations and a pedicure manicure area within an environment that is like no other.

At Moxie you will quickly see that we are not only an amazing team of talented stylists, but a very close family. You will feel at home the second you walk in and be planning your next visit to Moxie while thinking of all the friends you want to share this amazing discovery with.

Kari
Kari Lisak is proud to be the owner of Moxie and to have created a unique environment with an abundance of style, passion and talent. She has had an amazing 19 years of hair styling. With an art background, Kari thinks of every hairstyle and color application as an art piece. Dry cutting and free hand hair coloring are a few favorites among her many specialities. Having a love for beautiful, stylish, long hair, she has built a trust with many clients that cherish their long hair and appreciate her understanding of it. She wants every client to feel at home at Moxie and makes that her priority.
Julz

With over 10 years experience, she makes it her priority to seek top results. She specializes in anything from traditional to edgy and is certified for numerous types of hair extensions and the Brazillion Blowout Treatment. Julz will not only give you expert advice and custom design the look you want, but also develop a personal relationship with you as her client.

Jenifer

Brandy

Nathan
Nathan is a dynamic Hair Stylist with extensive experience in specialized beauty care including hair cutting, coloring (Wella and Keune), extensions, dreads, braiding, weaves, color mixing with synthetic hair, hair piece analysis and placement, up-do's and make-up. Trained at The Vidal Sassoon Academy he has been doing hair for over 10 years. He is now an international platform artist with Keratin Complex and involved with the launch of their new color line. Nathan also works for the New York Blow Dry Bar in the meat packing district, and has been educating salons across the country on how to perfect the "Art of the perfect blow dry." He has also worked for Moroccan Oil, Christophe Salon in Beaverly Hills and John of Italy. Nathan loves every aspect of the hair industry, he believes that hair is a definite art form.
Blayke

Starr
Starr a gifted and talented stylist has been exercising her talents with hair for over 5 years. From short sassy curls to long sleek layers she loves working with all textures, colors and styles.
