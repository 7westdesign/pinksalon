Welcome to Moxie Hair & Design!
H aving always dreamed of working in an environment that captured her love for art, design, and hair, Kari Staben Lisak created Moxie:

Our Story

 It started out as a boutique salon with just four stations. Then, word quickly spread of the unique salon in Camarillo with an environment truly like no other. Within just a few months, the staff doubled in size - Growing from just four stylists to eight. With the blessing of having more stylists and business than the space could handle, Kari made the decision to expand to the building next door, more than doubling the space! Now, just a few years and more than three stations later, the Moxie family has grown to 18 stylists, 13 unique styling stations, and a special new pedicure and manicure area, designed for you.

Your Moxie

 Moxie's environment is truly original. When entering, you'll probably notice our custom cotton candy machine, a fine selection of fine beer and wine on tap, and an amazing assortment of treasures, vintage design, and art. You'll get the feel early on that you are not only in the company of an amazing team of talented stylists, but also a very close family. You will feel at home the second you walk in and hopefully be planning your next visit to Moxie while thinking of all the friends you wish to share your amazing discovery with. At Moxie, we take care of you - you'll most likely leave with a smile or perhaps a cotton candy or vintage drink. But most importantly you'll leave with an amazing styling experience to cherish forever.

Love, Kari and Moxie Family
